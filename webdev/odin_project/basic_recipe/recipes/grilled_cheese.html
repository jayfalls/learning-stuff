Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

Grilled cheese is a classic.

Ingredients

  • Cheese - We used sharp cheddar
  • Onion - We used yellow onion, or sweet onion works well
  • Garlic cloves - You can add more if you love garlic
  • Red wine (or beef broth) - This amps up the flavor of your sauce (avoid using cooking wine).
  • Bread - We used whole wheat
  • Butter - We used unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Add the cheese
  3. Add the onion
  4. Add the garlic
  5. Toast the bread
